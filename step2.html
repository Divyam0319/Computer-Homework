Step 2 - AVOID AIR, WATER AND LAND POLLUTION
MORE STEPS

Air pollution have shown a great effect on plantations, health and life, decreasing the enjoyment of living, rising medical costs, causes global warming and also causes the destruction of the ozone layer. Water pollution changes the taste of water and brings about harmful chemical changes. Water pollution harms marine life and causes serious illnesses when drunk. Besides, some of the causes of land pollution are the use of pesticides, agricultural and industrial waste, deforestation, growing urbanization, acid rains, and mining activities. Furthermore, these activities not only cause damage to the soil but also the reason for various human and animal infections and diseases.

TABLE: Ways To Avoid Air, Water and Land Pollution
WAYS PICTURES
1) Do not throw garbage on streets or at the roadsides.
2) Avoid water to be collected anywhere otherwise it will became a breeding source for mosquitos.
3) Do not throw the toxic and harmful factory wastes into the water bodies.
4) Charge a huge fine on the vehicles releasing harmful gases and smoke, factories releasing smoke and on smoking.
5) Avoid bonfires during winters.
6) Minimize the use of chemical fertilisers and pesticides in the fields and instead use compost.
